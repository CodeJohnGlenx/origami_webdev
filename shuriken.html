Shuriken

Originating from the secretive world of the Japanese ninja, shuriken were traditionally used as concealed weapons for self-defense and surprise attacks. Over time, these ninja stars have captured the imaginations of many, becoming iconic symbols of the art of the ninja. In recent years, the concept of creating these deadly weapons in a harmless, artistic paper form through Origami has gained popularity among enthusiasts and hobbyists alike.
